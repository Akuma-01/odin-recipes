Peri Peri Biryani

This peri peri chicken is made with my take on African peri peri sauce using fresh and dried chiles. The marinade is incredibly flavorful and gives the chicken a beautiful color as well.

Ingredients

  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 4 chicken leg/thigh quarters
  • 4 red Fresno chili peppers, seeded
  • kosher salt to taste to season chicken
  • 2 lemons, juiced

Steps

  1. Combine garlic, Fresno chili peppers, habanero pepper, bell pepper, smoked paprika, thyme, kosher salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, sugar, and onion powder in a blender.
  2. Pour 3/4 of the marinade into resealable plastic bag set in a bowl. Reserve the rest for serving on the side.
  3. Make 3 or 4 shallow cuts into the skin side of the chicken quarters. This will allow the sauce to penetrate the skin during marination. Season chicken on both sides with kosher salt
  4. Preheat an outdoor charcoal grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Remove chicken from marinade. Set skinside down onto the hot grill, using indirect heat. Cover to keep coals from getting too hot. Cook covered for about 5 minutes, then turn them over and grill another 10 to 12 minutes.
  6. Serve with reserved sauce.
